Ping to the Pong!

Enter a number between 1-60 to get a Ping Pong!

A couple of rules to remeber

  1. Positive numbers only from 1-60
  2. Numbers that are multiples of 3's will result in a Ping!
  3. Numbers that are multiples of 5's will result in a Pong!!
  4. Numbers that are multiples of 15 will result in a PING PONG!!!!
